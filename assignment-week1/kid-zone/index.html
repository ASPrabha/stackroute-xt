KidZone is the largest and one of the most profitable preschool chains in Asia with more than 1700+ centers in over 550+ cities across the country. Within almost a decade of its existence, KidZone has touched the lives of more than 450,000 children. Apart from the positive impact that KidZone brings in the lives of its business partners, we also empower them to bring positive changes in the lives of our teachers and numerous others.

To qualify to be a KidZone school franchise, you need to have a property with minimum area of 2000-3000 sq. feet and a minimum investment capacity of Rs. 12, 00,000. KidZone is looking for passionate entrepreneurs who are committed and willing to promote quality education and help in shaping the future of the nation.
